Foundations

Spacing

A 4px base unit. Every gap, pad, and margin in the product is a step on this scale — no magic numbers. Denser controls use 1–3; layout rhythm uses 4–12.

Foundations

Radius

Rounded but precise — a balanced geometry that reads as crafted, not toy-like. Controls use sm–md, cards use lg–xl, pills and avatars use full.

Foundations

Elevation

Six Material-style levels. In light mode, layered soft shadows; in dark mode, shadows deepen and surfaces lighten tonally so depth survives. Resting cards sit at 1, menus at 3, dialogs at 4–5.

Foundations

Motion

Snappy and purposeful — 150–300ms, standard easing for most UI, emphasized for entrances, spring for playful affordances. Everything respects prefers-reduced-motion. Click a track to replay.
